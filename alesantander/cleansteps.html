Role: Ux | Ui Designer

Taking the helm as the Ux | Ui Designer for a dynamic start-up offering pick-up and delivery services for wash and fold laundry in the USA was a challenging yet rewarding experience. My responsibilities extended from initial design concepts, wireframes, and design system creation to close collaboration with the development team, utilizing agile methodologies and design sprints for an efficient and cohesive development process.

Project Scope

Objective:

Craft a user-friendly and visually engaging app for efficient pick-up and delivery laundry services in the USA.

Responsibilities:

Led the design process, from wireframes to a comprehensive design system and individual components. Collaborated closely with the development team, ensuring seamless integration of design elements into the app's functionality.

Agile Methodologies

Process:

Embraced agile methodologies and design sprints, fostering efficient communication and collaboration between design and development teams. This iterative approach allowed for swift adjustments based on ongoing feedback, ensuring a streamlined and effective development process.

Key Achievements

User-Centric Design:

Prioritized user experience in every design decision, resulting in an app that not only met but exceeded user expectations.

Efficient Collaboration:

The close collaboration with the development team facilitated a smooth transition from design concepts to a fully functional app, enhancing the overall project efficiency.

Technologies Used
  • Design: Sketch App, Invision App and Adobe Creative Suite

This project underscores my proficiency in designing user-centric apps and highlights my collaborative approach with development teams. The use of agile methodologies and design sprints not only streamlined the development process but also reflects my commitment to efficient and effective project execution. As I transition towards becoming a Front-End Developer, this project stands as a testament to my evolving skill set.
