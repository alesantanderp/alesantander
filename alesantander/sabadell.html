Role: Designer & Front-End Developer

Taking the reins as the Designer and Front-End Developer for Banc Sabadell's Branded Content project was a thrilling challenge. Tasked with creating a captivating website hosting 10 custom-made content pieces and a dedicated HUB for the series, my role extended beyond design to managing stakeholder communication and orchestrating collaboration with a talented illustrator.

Project Scope

Objective:

Craft a website to showcase 10 unique custom made content pieces for Banc Sabadell's project. Develop a content HUB for a series consolidation, enhancing user engagement.

Responsibilities:

As the Designer, I conceptualized the website's aesthetics and collaborated with an illustrator for added visual appeal. Developed a comprehensive proposal based on the client brief, including wireframes, design mockups, and user flow, creating a roadmap for the website's structure and functionality. Managing the entire process, I also took charge of Front-End development using JavaScript libraries like GSAP as a framework to optimize the delivering time and process.

Stakeholder Management

Orchestrated effective communication with Banc Sabadell, ensuring alignment with their vision and goals throughout the design and development process.
