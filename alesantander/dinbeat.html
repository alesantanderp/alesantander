Dinbeat

It's alive

Role: Brand Identity Designer, Product Designer, and Front-End Developer

Embarking on a transformative journey with Dinbeat, a pioneering startup in pet healthcare, I took on the multifaceted role of Brand Identity Designer, Product Designer, and Front-End Developer. This comprehensive project aimed to introduce a revolutionary health wearable for pets, monitored through a software platform designed specifically for veterinarians.

Brand Identity Design

Objective:

Establish a distinctive brand identity for Dinbeat across various touchpoints, including logo construction, color schemes, applications, packaging, and brand promotion.

Execution:

From crafting the logo that encapsulates Dinbeat's essence to choosing colors that resonate with the target audience, every aspect of the brand identity was meticulously designed to convey innovation and trust.

Product Design & Development

Objective:

Create an intuitive software platform for veterinarians, seamlessly integrating with Dinbeat's health wearable for pets.

Process:

Conducted user testing and interviews with veterinarians to understand their needs. Developed wireframes and design systems to ensure a user-friendly interface. Implemented Front-End development to bring the software to life.

The Challenge

Dinbeat has consistently enhanced its primary product for veterinarians, leading to widespread popularity.

Numerous new clients, impressed by the product, expressed that it should be accessible to every veterinarian in the country.

In line with market trends, the company decided to focus on creating a solution tailored to veterinary hospitals. Consequently, they required a scalable product to ensure that the B2C market could enjoy the experience they deserve.

Problem statement

Currently, there is a gap in the market for a user-friendly solution that seamlessly integrates with Dinbeat's pet health wearable, addressing the specific workflow requirements of veterinarians.

The project aims to bridge this gap by conducting user testing, creating wireframes, and implementing Front-End development to deliver a comprehensive software platform that enhances the efficiency and effectiveness of pet healthcare.

Ux Design

Gathered insights through user testing, interviews, user flows and prototyping to start the design process and documenting everything.

Ui Design

Carefully crafted user interface, incorporating wireframes and design systems to ensure a seamless and user-friendly experience.

Development

Brought the design to life, providing vets with a tailored solution that enhances their workflow and optimizes pet healthcare management.

The end platform for vets includes all the necessary features. The vet can analyze all the health parameters of their patients, the record of each of demand cross the data.

Also all the registered patient information is accessible from the dashboard. A hospital will just have to pay just one time for the product.

Dashboard

Easy to use and visually appealing platform for patient management.

Engagement

Clear insight into a patient evolution.

Recording

Information about patients and all of their record while using the Dinbeat UNO in one place.

Data

All the data accessible from the platform.

Comprehensive Design Approach:

Taking charge of the entire design spectrum, I ensured a cohesive and visually compelling brand presence, from digital interfaces to tangible products like packaging and branding application.

User-Centric Software:

The software platform was not just a technological feat but a user-centric solution, shaped by insights gathered through extensive interactions with veterinarians.

Technologies Used
  • Brand Identity: Adobe Creative Suite, Wordpress
  • Product Design & Front-End Development: Sketch, InVision, UXPin and Visual Studio Code
  • Programming languages: HTML, and CSS

This project showcases my ability to seamlessly blend creativity with functionality, spanning brand identity creation, product design, and the technical implementation of software interfaces. The impact is reflected in the harmonious blend of design and technology that defines Dinbeat's visual and functional identity.
