WTR

Branding Wordpress Web Design
It's alive

Role: Brand Identity Designer & Web Developer

Embarking on a freelance endeavor, I had the privilege of creating the entire brand identity for 'Welding to Recycle' (WTR), a company dedicated to repurposing containers for building sustainable homes, offices, and commercial spaces. From logo design to the comprehensive brand image, my creative journey extended to developing their WordPress website, customizing it to align with their unique identity.

Brand Identity Design

Objective:

Craft a distinctive brand image for 'Welding to Recycle' (WTR), emphasizing sustainability and innovation.

Execution:

As the sole designer, I conceptualized and executed the logo and all elements of the brand identity, mirroring the company's commitment to recycling containers for construction purposes.

Website Development

Objective:

Develop an engaging WordPress website that mirrors WTR's values and showcases their projects.

Process:

Customized a WordPress theme to seamlessly integrate WTR's brand aesthetic. This involved not only implementing design elements but also considering user experience and functionality.

Key Achievements

Holistic Brand Presence:

The brand identity I crafted became the visual anchor for WTR, reflecting their commitment to sustainability and innovation across all touchpoints.

WordPress Customization:

Utilized my web design skills to customize a WordPress theme, ensuring the website not only looked appealing but also functioned seamlessly for users.

Technologies Used
  • Brand Identity Design: Adobe Creative Suite
  • Web Development: Figma, WordPress, HTML, CSS, JavaScript

This project encapsulates my versatility as a designer and emerging skills as a web developer. From defining a unique brand identity to translating it into a functional and visually appealing website, the project not only showcases my design prowess but also hints at my transition towards becoming a Front-End Developer.
