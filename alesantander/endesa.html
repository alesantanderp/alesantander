Role: Designer & Front-End Developer

In this dynamic collaboration between La Vanguardia and Endesa, I took the helm as the Designer and Front-End Developer, overseeing the creation of a compelling website for a Branded Content project. Endesa, being one of the largest electric utility companies in Spain, entrusted us to convey their message with impact.

Project Scope

Objective:

Develop a website showcasing 8 custom-made content pieces for the campaign, seamlessly blending the brand aesthetics of La Vanguardia and Endesa.

Responsibilities:

As the sole Designer, I conceptualized and executed the website's visual elements. Additionally, I took charge of the Front-End development, ensuring a smooth and engaging user experience.

Illustrations:

Crafting bespoke illustrations to complement the campaign's narrative, infusing a unique visual identity into the digital space.

Key Achievements

User Engagement:

The website garnered an impressive 150k users views, attesting to its effectiveness in capturing audience attention.

Cross-Platform Optimization:

Implemented responsive design principles for editorial content, ensuring a seamless experience across various devices, from desktops to mobiles.

Collaborative Innovation:

Working closely with La Vanguardia and Endesa teams, our collaboration resulted in a harmonious fusion of brand messaging, design aesthetics, and technological functionality.

Implementing responsive design ensured a user-centric experience, enabling seamless engagement with Endesa's content on any device. It preserved the brand's identity, adapting to various screen sizes for a cohesive appearance.

Technologies Used
  • Programming languages: HTML, CSS and JS for Front-End Development
  • Figma and Visual Studio Code for Web Design and prototyping
  • Adobe Creative Suite and Procreate for Graphic Design and Illustrations

This project exemplifies my transition from a Web Designer to a Front-End Developer, showcasing my proficiency in both design aesthetics and the technical aspects of web development.
